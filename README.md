# LEAVE HR APP — Support

**LEAVE HR APP** is a mobile application for employees to request and manage leave through your organization’s HR systems.

If you need help using the app, signing in, or linking your employee account, use the contacts below.

---

## Common topics

- **Sign-in or password issues** — Contact your HR or IT administrator if your organization manages accounts centrally. You can also email us using the address above.
- **Linking your employee account** — Use the in-app **Link Employee** flow; if it fails, email us with the error message shown on screen (if any).
- **Leave requests or balances** — Data is provided by your employer’s HR system; for policy questions, contact your HR department.
